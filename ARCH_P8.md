## Purpose

Phase VIII starts from the completed Phase VII evidence run at:

- `/home/josh/Documents/GitHub/GeometricUnity/reports/post_phase7_evidence_campaign/20260315T130526Z`

Phase VII improved realism and specificity, but it did not yet cross the final

again, not rebuilding the reporting shell.

## Verified Starting State

The current repository now proves all of the following from generated artifacts:

- the standard Phase V campaign runs cleanly from strict validation through dossier and report output,
- the standard branch/refinement path consumes a real persisted atlas and records source state artifacts in the bridge manifest,
- the scorecard is environment-aware and records which environment supplied each match,
- the standard sidecar path records origin by channel and no longer uses heuristic channel records,
- the standard run preserves negative results,
- a linked `first-order-curvature` Shiab companion run exists.

## Architectural Boundary

The remaining boundary is no longer "can the pipeline run?" It is "does the
pipeline carry nontrivial, external, and candidate-specific evidence?"

The decisive remaining limits are:

- the standard atlas is real but still a trivial toy-control zero-seed residual-inspection atlas,
- the imported environment is still synthetic-example provenance,
- three of four sidecar channels are still bridge-derived rather than upstream evidence products,
- the harder quantitative benchmark is still internal rather than external,
- claim escalation still shares too much global campaign success across candidates,
- Shiab scope expansion exists only as a linked trivial-control companion run.

## Workstreams

### W1. Replace Trivial-Control Atlas Evidence With Nontrivial Upstream Solves

Required outcome:

- the standard campaign consumes a persisted atlas produced from non-zero seeds or replayed seeds,
- the atlas records are no longer classified as trivial residual-inspection runs,
- branch and convergence claims are backed by nontrivial upstream solve outputs.

### W2. Replace Synthetic Imported Geometry With A Genuine Dataset

Required outcome:

- the imported tier points to a real dataset with concrete provenance,
- copied campaign inputs and dossiers preserve that provenance,
- imported-tier comparisons can be interpreted as real external evidence.

### W3. Promote Remaining Bridge-Derived Sidecars To Upstream Evidence Products

Required outcome:

- observation-chain records come from persisted upstream observation artifacts,
- representation-content records come from explicit upstream family or representation artifacts,
- environment-variance records come from actual per-environment quantitative runs rather than bridge summaries.

### W4. Make Claim Escalation Candidate-Specific

Required outcome:

- branch, convergence, quantitative, and sidecar gates join to candidate-specific evidence identifiers,
- promotions and demotions are driven by the evidence that actually belongs to each candidate.

### W5. Move Shiab Expansion Into The Main Evidence Path

Required outcome:

- a non-identity Shiab branch appears in the main standard campaign or in a standard paired campaign,
- the run uses nontrivial inputs,
- the final evaluation states whether robustness and convergence conclusions survive that broader scope.

### W6. Separate Internal Benchmarks From External Evidence

Required outcome:

- artifacts clearly distinguish control targets, internal harder benchmarks, and genuine external measurements,
- headline interpretation does not blur those categories.

## Exit Criteria

Phase VIII is complete only when all of the following are true:

- the standard campaign is backed by nontrivial upstream solve artifacts,
- imported-tier evidence refers to a real dataset,
- remaining bridge-derived sidecars are replaced by upstream evidence products,
- claim escalation is candidate-specific,
- Shiab scope expansion is exercised on nontrivial evidence,
- internal and external benchmarks are clearly separated in artifacts and summaries.
