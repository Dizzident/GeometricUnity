## Summary

This cluster covers electroweak superconnection models in which gauge fields and
the Higgs field are assembled into a single generalized connection. The route is
geometrically relevant because it treats the Higgs as part of connection data
rather than as an unrelated inserted scalar and because the graded SU(2/1)
embedding constrains electroweak normalizations, the weak mixing angle, and
tree-level W/Z/H mass ratios.

The direct numerical lead is strong enough to track carefully. Roepstorff's
superconnection treatment records a tree-level relation
`sin^2(theta)=1/4` and the mass-squared ratio
`m_W^2:m_Z^2:m_H^2=3:4:12`. Loginov's SU(2/1) gauge-Higgs route records a
different tree-level normalization and loop-corrected Higgs values near the
observed Higgs mass. Aydemir, Minic, Sun, and Takeuchi document that the
minimal SU(2/1) superconnection route gives a higher Higgs estimate, while a
related SU(2/2) left-right extension can accommodate a near-126 GeV Higgs.

## Prediction Relevance

The route is relevant because it is one of the clearest external examples of a
geometric electroweak source structure: it embeds electroweak gauge data in a
graded algebra, treats the Higgs as connection data, and can produce W/Z/H
ratio or Higgs-mass claims under model-specific assumptions.

That still does not complete this repository's prediction contract. The route
does not supply a GU-local theorem mapping GU fields to observed photon/W/Z/H
rows, separate W and Z source rows, target-independent GU VEV or weak-scale
lineage, GU weak-angle and gauge-coupling normalization, observed-field
extraction, Higgs scalar-source/self-coupling lineage, or GeV normalization.
The near-observed Higgs claims also depend on model choices, loop effective
potential/RG treatment, electroweak matching, and observed Standard Model
inputs.

## Limitation

Treat as an external geometric ratio and Higgs-as-connection lead, not as a
prediction source. It cannot fill Phase201, Phase202, or Phase256 unless a
separate GU-local bridge theorem maps the superconnection structure into the
repository's observed-field and source-lineage rows without importing target
mass values.
