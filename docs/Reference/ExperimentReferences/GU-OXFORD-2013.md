## Summary

The Oxford lecture is an official public GU presentation. It gives conceptual
context for why GU treats the Standard Model, gauge structure, and Higgs-sector
questions as geometric problems. It has been used as a high-level guide to what a
successful source law should accomplish.

- Supported Phase368's focused audit of the public inhomogeneous/tilted gauge,
  swervature/displasion, generalized Yang-Mills, and Dirac-square equation
  scaffold.

  replayable physical mass extraction.

## Prediction Relevance

GU-native equation scaffold material. It does not by itself provide the W/Z mass
split, weak-mixing-angle source, VEV source, Higgs scalar source, Higgs
