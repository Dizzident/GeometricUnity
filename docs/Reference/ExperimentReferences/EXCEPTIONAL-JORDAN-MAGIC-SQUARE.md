## Summary

This source family uses exceptional Jordan algebra, the Albert algebra,
Peirce/triality structure, Jordan geometry, and Freudenthal-Tits magic-square
ideas to encode Standard Model symmetries, generations, electroweak subgroups,
Higgs/Yukawa structure, and in the current Singh paper, charged-fermion mass
ratios.

The route is distinct from the existing E8 and octonion/Clifford audits. It is
closer to internal-space representation geometry and generation structure than
to a worked electroweak physical mass extraction.
