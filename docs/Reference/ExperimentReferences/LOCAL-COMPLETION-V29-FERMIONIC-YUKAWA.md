## Summary

The v29 completion document makes a previously implicit fermionic/Yukawa
research gap auditable. It records a typed fermionic-action template with a
branch-local Dirac-like operator and Yukawa-like lower-order term, but explicitly
classifies that action as a placeholder rather than a completed derivation.

`VO-6`, leaves the coupled boson-fermion mixed-linearization blocks and
gauge-compatibility identities under `VO-7`, and keeps fermionic coupling
outside the first executable numerical branch.

## Prediction Relevance

The document identifies a credible next derivation program: construct a
GU-native fermionic action, solve its Yukawa-like coupling map, derive the mixed
boson-fermion Hessian blocks, and extract a physical scalar row.

It does not itself provide a solved Higgs scalar-source operator, identity
envelope, massive scalar profile, quartic or excitation relation, target-blind
prediction row, observed-field extraction, pole extraction, or GeV
normalization. It also does not supply a direct W/Z absolute-mass theorem.

not merely the typed placeholders.
