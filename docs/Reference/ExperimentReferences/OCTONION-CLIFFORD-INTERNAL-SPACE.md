## Summary

through octonions, Clifford algebras, particle-subspace projectors, and
division-algebraic ladder operators. The strongest boson-relevant source is the
octonion internal-space paper, which uses a preferred complex structure in

identifies the Standard Model gauge group as the subgroup preserving a sterile
neutrino vacuum and treats the Higgs field as the scalar part of a

`m_H^2 / m_W^2 = 5/2`, also written using a theoretical Weinberg angle. This is
a close ratio-level diagnostic lead but not a physical W/Z/H prediction in this
