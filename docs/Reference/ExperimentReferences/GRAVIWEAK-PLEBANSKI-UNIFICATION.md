## Summary

This cluster covers graviweak and extended-Plebanski routes in which gravity,
weak SU(2), Yang-Mills fields, and Higgs-like fields arise from an enlarged
connection or gauge-gravity action. The original Nesti-Percacci route identifies
weak SU(2) with a chiral component of the Lorentz/graviweak connection. Later
Spin(4,4) models use extended Plebanski actions and symmetry breaking to recover
gravity, SU(2) Yang-Mills, and Higgs-sector actions.

The route is geometrically relevant because it is not just a Standard Model
formula replay. It puts weak gauge fields and gravitational connection data into
a common gauge-geometric setting and treats the Higgs sector through frame-Higgs
or vacuum-breaking structure. The Multiple Point Principle branch also quotes a
Higgs-mass interval of 125-143 GeV, but that is tied to SM effective-potential
criticality rather than a GU-local scalar-source derivation.

## Prediction Relevance

The route gives a useful diagnostic: a geometric unification can recover
gravity, weak SU(2), Yang-Mills, and Higgs-sector structures and still not
produce observed W/Z/H masses. In the Nesti-Percacci route, the W fields remain
massless until a lower-energy electroweak-breaking mechanism is introduced. In
the Spin(4,4) route, bare couplings and constants are tied to a unification
parameter and Higgs VEVs, with Planck-scale matching and running required before
low-energy comparison.

That does not complete this repository's prediction contract. The route does
not supply GU-local W/Z source rows, target-independent GU VEV or weak-scale
lineage, GU weak-angle and gauge-coupling normalization, observed photon/W/Z/H
projection, neutral mass-matrix diagonalization, Higgs scalar-source and
self-coupling lineage, or GeV normalization.

## Limitation

Treat as an external gauge-gravity-Higgs unification lead, not as a prediction
source. The route depends on a specific enlarged gauge group, a symmetry-breaking
ansatz, a Higgs VEV or curvature vacuum, electroweak-breaking completion,
Planck-scale bare-parameter matching, RG transport, and Standard Model
completion. Those dependencies are not the same as GU-local source-lineage rows.
