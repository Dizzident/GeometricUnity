## Summary

This source restricts the `Z2`-graded Clifford tensor product
`Cl4 hat-tensor Cl6` to a particle subspace, promotes weak hypercharge to a
superselection rule, and defines the Higgs field as the scalar part of
Quillen's superconnection. It also excludes the zero-hypercharge sterile
neutrino subspace from the particle projector, producing an `sl(2|1)` style
superalgebra in the route described by the paper.

The boson-relevant result is the relation
`m_H = 2 cos(theta_W) m_W = sqrt(5/2) m_W`, obtained by equating
superconnection normalizations in the lepton and quark subalgebras. This is a
serious Higgs/W ratio lead, but it is still an external algebraic construction,
not a GU-local physical mass prediction.
