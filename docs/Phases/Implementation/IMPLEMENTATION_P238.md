# Phase 238: Cox II Ready-to-Fit Formula Dependency Audit

## Purpose

Audit whether Cox GU II Appendix S ready-to-fit formulas for mixings and masses close the repository's W/Z/H target-independent prediction requirements, or whether they remain parameterized fit relations.

## Result

The formulas are useful phenomenological relations, but they are not promotable as boson mass predictions. They depend on fit/source parameters such as `g_L`, `g_R`, `g_B-L`, `kappa`, `beta`, `v_R`, `g_4`, `v_4`, and `c_X`; the repository still lacks independent GU source rows, RG/threshold transport, observed W/Z rows, and a Higgs scalar-source/potential package.
