# Implementation P231: External Cox GU Paper I Source-Intake Audit

P231 audits an external 2025 GU-related preprint as possible new source-lineage evidence:

`Geometric Unity I: From Heuristic Proposal to Testable Framework. Shiab Uniqueness, Invariant Curvature, Augmented Torsion, and Projection-Variation with Boundary Control`, Joseph Thomas Cox, DOI `10.5281/zenodo.17252989`.

The phase treats the paper as a useful external research lead for Shiab uniqueness, invariant curvature, augmented torsion, and projection-variation machinery. It does not treat the paper as an automatic boson-mass prediction source.

The audit maps the paper against the existing Phase209 W/Z and Higgs evidence requests. It remains fail-closed because the current external paper does not fill W/Z particle rows, raw-amplitude gates, common-bridge gates, Higgs scalar-source fields, stability sidecars, physical mass-matrix extraction, GU-derived VEV source, or target-comparison gates. It also records that relevant coupling constants and phenomenology are downstream work, not completed W/Z/H mass predictions in the current paper.
