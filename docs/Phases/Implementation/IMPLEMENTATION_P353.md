# Phase353 Gauge-Higgs Unification Source Audit

Phase353 records gauge-Higgs/Hosotani unification as a focused negative
boson-source audit. The route is relevant because it is a direct geometric
mass-generation lead: the Higgs degree of freedom can be treated as an
extra-dimensional gauge component, and electroweak breaking can be tied to a
Wilson-line/Aharonov-Bohm phase.

The audit is intentionally non-promotional. The reviewed sources depend on an
external Randall-Sundrum extra-dimensional model, gauge-group choice,
orbifold/boundary data, brane or boundary breaking, KK scale, Wilson-line
phase, bulk spectrum, and precision matching. They do not supply a GU-local
map, target-independent Wilson-line or KK-scale source, observed-field
extraction, Higgs scalar-source operator, or GeV normalization.
