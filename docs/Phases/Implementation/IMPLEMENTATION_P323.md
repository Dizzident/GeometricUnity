# Phase323 Coupled Yang-Mills-Higgs Mass Extraction Audit

Phase323 audits the remaining combined loophole after the neutral W/Z and
Higgs/Upsilon boundary checks: whether official GU placement of weak isospin,
weak hypercharge, Higgs field, Higgs potential, and Yang-Mills/Higgs equations
can be read together with the standard Higgs-mechanism dependency shape as a
complete W/Z/H mass extraction source.

The audit preserves the positive source-location evidence while keeping it
separate from a promotable prediction. The public GU material gives locations
and motivation, and the Standard Model gives the external dependency map. A
prediction still requires a GU-derived electroweak vacuum/VEV, gauge-coupling
and weak-angle normalization, gauge-fixed quadratic expansion, photon/W/Z/H
projection rows, GeV unit normalization, and solved Higgs scalar
self-coupling/source lineage.

## Decision

Phase323 is expected to pass only as a negative boundary audit. Official GU
field/equation locations plus the external Standard Model mass-dependency map
do not fill the Phase201 W/Z or Higgs source-lineage contracts, and they do not
fill the Phase256 observed-field extraction contract.
