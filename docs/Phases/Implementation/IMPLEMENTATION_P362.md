# Phase362 Framed Standard Model Source Audit

Phase362 audits framed gauge theory and the Framed Standard Model as a possible
W/Z/H geometric source-law lead.

The phase records sources where internal symmetry-space frame vectors are
promoted to field variables, called framons, and where the electroweak Higgs is
identified with the electroweak framon. It also tracks FSM generation symmetry,
rotating rank-one fermion mass matrices, the theta-to-CKM phase lead, and the
gamma-Z-G vector-mixing phenomenology.

The audit is intentionally non-promotional. The sources do not supply a
GU-local framed-gauge map from Shiab or observer-sector geometry, a
target-independent framon vacuum or potential, separate W/Z source rows,
observed photon/W/Z/H projection, Higgs scalar-source and self-coupling
lineage, a VEV or mass-scale source, low-energy RG/threshold and pole
extraction, or GeV normalization.
