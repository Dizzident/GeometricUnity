# Phase356 Eguchi-Hanson Substandard Higgs Source Audit

Phase356 records the Eguchi-Hanson "Substandard Theory" Higgs relation as a
focused negative boson-source audit. The lead is relevant because it offers a
geometric/algebraic interpretation of an electroweak-like `U(2)` sector and
reports an explicit Higgs relation using the W mass and Weinberg angle.

The audit is intentionally non-promotional. The reviewed source imports the W
mass and weak-mixing angle, does not predict W/Z absolute masses or the weak
angle, gives a Higgs estimate near 115.3 GeV rather than the observed 125 GeV
scale, excludes QCD/quark-sector completion, and supplies no GU-local
Eguchi-Hanson map, observed-field extraction, W/Z source rows, Higgs
scalar-source or self-coupling lineage, target-independent scale, or GeV
normalization.
