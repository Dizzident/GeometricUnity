Yang-Mills-Higgs reduced-phase-space, and Higgs-branch symplectic-singularity
sources as possible W/Z/H geometric source-law leads.

symplectic phase spaces, where Yang-Mills theory is treated through moment-map
geometry, where singular symplectic cotangent-bundle reduction is applied to
the Glashow-Weinberg-Salam Higgs sector, and where partial Higgs mechanisms are

Hamiltonian reduction or reduced-vacuum selection law, observed photon/W/Z/H
projection, separate W/Z source rows, Higgs scalar-source and self-coupling
lineage, a VEV or mass-scale source, low-energy RG/threshold and pole
