# Phase355 Dirac-Lichnerowicz Yang-Mills-Higgs Source Audit

Phase355 records the generalized Dirac/Lichnerowicz/Yang-Mills-Higgs route as
a focused negative boson-source audit. The lead is relevant because it derives
gravity/Yang-Mills and Standard Model action structures from Dirac-type
operators, gives the Higgs sector a geometric interpretation in generalized
Dirac geometry, and includes a historical Dirac-type Higgs-mass estimate.

The audit is intentionally non-promotional. The reviewed sources depend on
external Clifford-module and Dirac-operator choices, Standard Model gauge
representations, Yukawa and fermionic mass-operator inputs, normalization
conventions, physical-subspace choices, one-loop/top-mass approximations,
electroweak matching, and observed comparison. They do not supply a GU-local
Dirac/Lichnerowicz map, Clifford-module bridge, W/Z source rows, Higgs
scalar-source or self-coupling lineage, observed-field extraction,
target-independent VEV/scale, or GeV normalization.
