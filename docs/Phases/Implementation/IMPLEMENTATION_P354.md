# Phase354 Multiplicative Higgs Lagrangian Source Audit

Phase354 records the inverse-variational multiplicative Higgs Lagrangian as a
focused negative boson-source audit. The lead is relevant because it modifies
the Higgs sector through a non-additive Lagrangian, preserves the Standard
Model electroweak structure in the appropriate limit, and organizes charged
fermion masses through finite scaling factors.

The audit is intentionally non-promotional. The reviewed sources depend on an
external nonstandard Higgs model, an auxiliary mass scale, epsilon/scaling
factor assignments, observed fermion pole masses, Standard Model gauge and
Yukawa structure, and SMEFT matching. They do not supply a GU-local
variational Lagrangian map, auxiliary-scale source, scaling-factor selection
law, W/Z source rows, Higgs scalar-source or self-coupling lineage,
observed-field extraction, target-independent VEV/scale, or GeV normalization.
