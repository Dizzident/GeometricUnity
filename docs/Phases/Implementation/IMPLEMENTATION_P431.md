# Implementation P431 - Lambda_8-Background Doublet-Reopening Probe

Phase431 (team wave) tests stage two of the Coleman-Weinberg program: in a
candidate lambda_8 background (t8 a RECORDED parameter, not dynamically
derived) the fermion spectrum is su(3)-broken, evading the Phase428
class-function theorem. Exact results (block spectrum verified vs dense at
4.4e-13; t8=0 reproduces Phase428's degeneracy at 3.6e-14): the T/D
distinction REOPENS (separation ~70-75 at t8 in {0.5,1,2}) and the
background INDUCES a block-dependent mass law with opposite signs (triplet
curvature positive/stabilizing, doublet negative/destabilizing at every
probed t8) - the structure Phase418 had to import externally. The gauge
sector shows the doublet direction gapped while the triplet is not.
Boundaries: t8 is candidate-only; no scale law; ordering is
parameter-dependent and NOT used to claim selection; no contract fill.
