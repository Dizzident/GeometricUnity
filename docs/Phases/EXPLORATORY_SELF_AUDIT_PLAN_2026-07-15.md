# Exploratory Self-Audit Plan (Amendment A5)

## Purpose

Make machine-executable progress while external review is deferred, without
weakening O4, Phase458, or any promotion boundary. The lane asks which results
survive adversarial convention changes and which assumptions remain matters of
physical interpretation.

## Evidence lanes

- **Exploration:** reduced, deterministic, target-blind tests; alternative
  conventions allowed; negative and failed artifacts preserved; no downstream
  scientific gate consumption.
- **Confirmation:** hypothesis, convention menu, estimator, thresholds, seeds,
  invalid-row policy, and terminal taxonomy frozen before decisive compute.
- **Promotion:** independent reproduction, external review, or an explicitly
  accepted convention-invariance derivation; source lineage remains mandatory.

Evidence may move only forward through a new prospective checkpoint. An
exploratory result can motivate confirmation but can never be relabeled as
confirmatory or promotional after inspection.

## First tranche

1. Phase484 freezes the governance firewall.
2. Phase485 maps every O4 item to a falsifier and evidence class.
3. Phase486 triages committed evidence and ranks the next reduced tests.

## Later work (not yet allocated)

- Exact/small-lattice controls for the theta measure and zero-mode menus.
- Independent-gradient and independent-implementation agreement batteries.
- Reduced deterministic checkpoint/restart equivalence before any expensive
  future sampler.
- Convention-family robustness tables for anchor-free dimensionless outputs.
- Prospective confirmation packs only for effects surviving the above.

External review remains necessary before community-facing physical
interpretation unless a later labeled amendment replaces a particular ruling
with stronger independent or theorem-grade evidence. No A5 phase modifies the
frozen Phase456 pack or authorizes Phase458 production.
