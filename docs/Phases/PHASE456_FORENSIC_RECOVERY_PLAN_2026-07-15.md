# Phase456 Forensic-Recovery Plan (Amendment A8)

## Purpose

Determine why the committed Phase456 production analysis failed before any
new compute is authorized. The original terminal and raw artifact are
immutable. This tranche is exploration-only and cannot rehabilitate a row.

## Phase493 — stored-artifact failure decomposition

- Bind the exact Phase456 source, analyzer, preregistration pack, production
  artifact, and summary bytes.
- Reconstruct every failed gate and row-domain condition independently where
  the retained artifact permits it.
- Separate sampler-control evidence, estimator-domain failure, effective-
  sample-size limitation, channel/observable validity, and family-firewall
  propagation.
- Emit one of: sampler-defect supported, estimator-domain failure supported,
  statistics-limited supported, mixed failure supported, failure source
  unresolved, or invalid committed artifact.

## Phase494 — estimator and oracle battery

- Freeze all estimators and thresholds before reading Phase493's terminal.
- Use deterministic analytic correlators and exact-Gaussian synthetic inputs;
  do not launch HMC or performance benchmarks.
- Test the original cosh estimator's domain and finite-volume identifiability,
  covariance-aware full-correlator residuals, target-blind alternative
  estimators, sign/positivity assumptions, and invalid-row propagation.
- Emit original estimator identifiable, alternative estimator feasible,
  channel nonidentifiable, mixed estimator outcome, or invalid oracle battery.

## Phase495 — prospective repair-readiness adjudicator

- Freeze and recheck exact Phase493/494 byte hashes and schemas.
- Apply conservative precedence across: invalid precursor, sampler
  implementation defect, estimator structurally unidentifiable, insufficient
  statistics, observable/channel invalid, prospective repair pack justified,
  and unresolved.
- A justified terminal means only that a new Phase481 pack may be designed
  after its existing authorization gates. It does not create that pack or
  authorize sampling.

Every branch is target-blind, workbench-relative, source-contract-ineligible,
and retains `promotedPhysicalMassClaimCount=0`.
