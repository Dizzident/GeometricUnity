# Phase416 - Unobserved-Phase Carrier Census

## Purpose

Materialize the restart prompt's post-Phase415 branch: determine whether the
draft's unobserved/dark sectors contain a computable carrier that can advance
the boson-prediction theory without importing observed W/Z/H targets.

## Result

- The primary draft does pin three unobserved/dark fermionic carrier families:
  `Q_{3/2}` with 192 states per displayed `zeta` side, `Z_{1/2}` with the
  vector-spinor `144` remainder and 576 states per side, and a dark decoupled
  Weyl-half/mirror sector with 64 states.
- The draft-side dimension check is exact: `64 + 192 + 576 = 832`.
- None of these source-pinned unobserved carriers is a linear bosonic spin-zero
  SM-doublet carrier.
- No current source supplies the missing bosonic map/action, VEV selection,
  observed photon/W/Z/H projection rows, weak-angle lineage, pole extraction,
  or GeV/unit normalization.
- The concrete next mathematical target is the `Z` / vector-spinor `144`
  decomposition plus any source-defined even composite or bosonic projection
  built from it.

## Boundary

contract field and promotes no W/Z/H mass claim.
