# Phase CXVII - W/Z Repaired Pair Sweep

Sweep all unordered Phase95 repaired exact fermion-mode pairs through the Phase84
W/Z replay route. The phase checks whether the Phase116 projection miss can be
repaired by choosing a different target-blind repaired fermion pair.

The scoring is intentionally target-blind for selection diagnostics: it records
raw matrix-element magnitude, W/Z common-bridge spread, and only then compares
the strongest admissible pair against the Phase110 target-implied raw magnitude.
