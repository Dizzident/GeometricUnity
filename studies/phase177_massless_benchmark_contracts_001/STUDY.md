# Phase 177 Massless Benchmark Contracts

This phase materializes benchmark contracts for massless photon and gluon rows without promoting any identity assignment.

The contracts define a dimensionless masslessness indicator target of zero for known massless gauge-boson benchmark rows. They do not derive a photon U(1) identity, a color-octet identity, or a physical mass-bound comparison. Downstream phases may use these contracts to remove the benchmark-contract blocker while preserving identity/source blockers.
