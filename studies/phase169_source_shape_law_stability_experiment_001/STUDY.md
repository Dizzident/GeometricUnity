# Phase 169 Source-Shape Law Stability Experiment

This phase tests whether the P167 source-shape correction can be made scientifically defensible by target-independent stability evidence.

It evaluates predeclared source-shape laws on the sibling Phase12 W/Z source backgrounds and checks whether the W/Z relative correction is stable across backgrounds. Stability is necessary but not sufficient: promotion also requires an analytic derivation of the law and downstream prediction validation.
