# Phase500: Phase456 adversarial prospective-acquisition stress test

## Scope

This phase exact-binds the Phase499 summary and freezes a deterministic,
target-blind synthetic stress family before reading its terminal. The frozen
family covers covariance-envelope shapes, autocorrelation/effective-sample-size
penalties, signed and invalid controls, a near-degenerate positive pair, a weak
positive secondary component, neighboring acquisition points around the
Phase497 planning point, recovery and false-selection thresholds, and seeds.

Every admitted scenario in every frozen covariance and autocorrelation row
must pass for a specification to be called robust. A point that passes only
the nominal positive one/two-component menu is assumption-conditional. Failure
of every point is retained as a first-class outcome.

The deterministic surrogate evaluates a frozen prospective estimator response
envelope; it is not production power, an empirical temporal extrapolation, or
a substitute for acquisition data. Phase499 is consumed only as a provenance
and validity precursor. Its retained `T=4` information does not tune the
already-frozen stress family.

## Verdict taxonomy

- `invalid-precursor-or-stress-battery`
- `robust-specification-identified`
- `assumption-conditional-specification`
- `no-viable-specification-within-audited-budget`

## Boundaries

This phase does not run HMC or a benchmark, mutate Phase456, construct or
authorize Phase481, authorize sampling, satisfy Phase458 G3/G5, discharge O4,
fill a source contract, or support a physical-unit claim. All results are
synthetic exploration-lane planning evidence, and
`promotedPhysicalMassClaimCount=0` on every branch.
