Repair the W/Z-route replay inputs by using the Phase95 target-blind matched
Phase94 exact projected fermion modes. This phase consumes Phase84 replay
outputs for the Phase12 W and Z source modes, checks that the raw matrix-element
evidence validates, and verifies that the selected fermion modes clear the
gauge-reduction, residual, branch-stability, and refinement-stability gates.

fermion-quality blocker needed before the absolute W/Z projection can be rerun.
