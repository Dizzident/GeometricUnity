# Phase505: Phase503 frozen failure localization

Phase505 exact-binds the committed Phase503 summary and accounts for all 11
failed operating rows without changing Phase503's thresholds, seeds, menu, or
negative result. Its taxonomy and the mirrored deterministic probability
formulas are frozen before the Phase503 bytes are consumed.

For each failed row, the phase records all failed gates and assigns a primary
localization. Failed decisive single-spectrum calls are false single-to-pair
calls. Failed decisive double-spectrum calls are unresolved pair ambiguity,
never successful selection. Coverage failures remain visible even when a
selection failure has higher precedence.

The exact expected probability is compared with the same frozen threshold as
the observed 24-replicate rate. This distinguishes a finite-grid marginal miss
from a structural operating-characteristic failure. It does not claim that a
marginal miss is harmless, nor does it overturn the observed gate failure.

This deterministic read-only diagnosis recommends no repair terminal, runs no
sampling or reprocessing, creates no pack, grants no authority, and makes no
physical claim. `promotedPhysicalMassClaimCount=0`.
