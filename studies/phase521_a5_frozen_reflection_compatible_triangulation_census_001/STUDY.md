# Phase521: Frozen Reflection-Compatible Triangulation Census

This deterministic A18 study freezes two geometry constructions, two
axis-zero reflection maps, and extents 3 and 4 before evaluating any result.
The committed lattice-canonical simplicial decomposition is the required
negative control. The audit-authored candidate is the order complex of the
nonempty cells of the periodic four-dimensional cubical cell poset.

Every check uses integer coordinates and exact set membership. For every
geometry, extent, and reflection, the study checks the vertex map, involution,
incidence, and total simplex closure separately in dimensions zero through
four. Restricted closure counts are reported but never promoted to total
closure.

Survival is finite-only. It does not select or register a geometry or
reflection, establish an orientation or all-volume theorem, mutate the
committed mesh, or establish reflection positivity. No sampling, production,
or physical-unit claim is authorized.
