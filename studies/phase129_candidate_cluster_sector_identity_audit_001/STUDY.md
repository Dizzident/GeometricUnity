# Phase129 Candidate Cluster Sector Identity Audit

This study audits whether the repaired quality fermion families can inherit a target-blind sector identity from their candidate/cluster-level evidence.

It follows the P125 source-family-enriched repaired modes into:

- Phase12 family clusters
- Phase5 observation-chain candidate links
- Phase4 unified fermion registry candidates
- Phase5 representation-content, coupling-consistency, and provenance records

Representation-content completeness is recorded, but it is not promoted as physical sector identity unless explicit `chargeSector`, weak-sector, or quantum-number labels are present.
