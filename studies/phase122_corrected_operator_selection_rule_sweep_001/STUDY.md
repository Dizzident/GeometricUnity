# Phase122 Corrected Operator Selection Rule Sweep

This phase follows the Phase121 analytic-variation repair by recomputing all Phase95 repaired fermion transitions directly against the corrected W/Z analytic variation matrices.

The goal is to determine whether the post-repair near-zero W/Z matrix elements are specific to pair `(0,3)` or are a broader selection-rule/cancellation issue across the target-blind repaired fermion basis.
