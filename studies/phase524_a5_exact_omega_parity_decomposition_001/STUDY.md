definitions, the curvature, contraction, mass, Lie-algebra, and mesh sources,

action contains the degree-three term `(t^3/2)<L,W>`. The frozen n=3 witness
assigns three integer su(2) vectors on the oriented boundary of face zero and

is exactly `1/2` and the exact rational action implied by the exact-bound

evenness for the identity member in that finite formula-bound scope. It is not a random or floating probe,
and no cross-face cancellation is assumed.

The independent-theta member retains the same structural omega degrees at
fixed theta, but its executable contraction uses binary64 inverse/exponential
coefficients. Phase524 does not infer an exact cancellation or refutation for
