# Phase384 Phase307 Basis-Energy Response-Image Proxy Audit

## Purpose

This study checks whether Phase27 per-candidate canonical `SU(2)` basis-energy
fractions provide a finer target-independent proxy that could rehabilitate the
Phase307 W/Z selector under the Phase379 suppressed-axis conflict.

## Boundary

The audit uses basis-energy fractions as a proxy only. It is not an observed
electroweak projection map, not a response-image projector applied to physical
W/Z fields, and not a W/Z source-law theorem.

## Expected Outcome

If every current Phase307 W source definition still carries substantial
suppressed-axis basis energy, then the Phase381/382/383 conflict is not repaired
by the existing Phase27 basis-energy metadata.
