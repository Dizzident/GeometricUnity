# Phase LII - Electroweak Absolute Scale Audit

Phase LI showed that broad boson predictions are blocked first by absolute
W/Z mass scale. Phase LII audits that blocker directly.

The current W and Z internal modes are validated, but a GeV conversion scale is
not. Two target-fit diagnostics were evaluated only as rejected controls:

- fitting the W target predicts Z at `91.36071050377454 GeV`;
- fitting the Z target predicts W at `80.2172681143632 GeV`.

Both controls are rejected because they fit one of the prediction targets. Their
cross-check residuals also expose the present W/Z ratio error in absolute-mass
units.

The phase adds a projection contract for the next implementation step. A valid
absolute-mass prediction must provide a replayable scale source that excludes
`physical-w-boson-mass-gev` and `physical-z-boson-mass-gev`, then project both
validated internal modes through the same scale with propagated uncertainty.
