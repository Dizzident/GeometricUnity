# Phase510: Phase481 Execution-Readiness Adjudicator

Phase510 is the dependent Amendment A14 adjudicator. It exact-binds Phases508
and 509, the reduced Phase489 restart control, and the Phase481 construction
plan. Before consuming them it freezes portable four-chain RNG semantics,
lossless retention arithmetic, aggregate-CPU cost refusal, fingerprint
mutation coverage, and fail-closed precedence.

The raw retention schema stores one site/face-type/channel tensor and derives
all temporal separations deterministically, avoiding an unintended additional
Cartesian-product copy. Its maximum uncompressed raw bounds are 25 GiB for
T16 and 100 GiB for T32; compression never weakens the refusal bound.

This phase performs deterministic contract tests only. It runs no sampler,
HMC, or benchmark, creates no Phase481 pack, cannot satisfy Phase480, and
preserves `promotedPhysicalMassClaimCount=0`.
