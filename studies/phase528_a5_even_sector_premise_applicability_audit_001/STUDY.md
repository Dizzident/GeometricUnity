# Phase 528 — A5 frozen even-sector premise-applicability audit

Phase528 is the second A20 leaf. It asks whether the committed A5 Stage-A pack's
explicit premise that the action is exactly even under `omega -> -omega` applies
to either member of the complete two-member executable Phase452 family.

The audit consumes only hash-bound committed artifacts. Phase524 supplies an
exact full-action odd witness for the identity member. Phase527 supplies an
exact full-action odd witness for the registered SD2 member on its theta-zero
slice. A single exact counterexample is sufficient to refute a global evenness
premise for the corresponding member.

The terminal finding is scoped to the current frozen A5 pack and registered
family. It is not a theorem about every possible action, does not establish or
refute reflection positivity, and does not provide a target-theorem
counterexample. No sampling, reprocessing, HMC, benchmark, production, source
contract application, physical-unit claim, or GeV claim is authorized.
