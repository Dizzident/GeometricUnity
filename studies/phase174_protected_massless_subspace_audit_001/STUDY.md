# Phase 174 Protected Massless Subspace Audit

This phase tests whether the Phase12 boson spectra support a target-independent protected massless gauge subspace.

It deliberately audits the whole zero-mode subspace instead of individual registry candidates. Individual candidates are branch-fragile, but the subspace dimension, eigenvalue envelope, gauge leak envelope, and convergence status can still establish a diagnostic massless-sector invariant. The result is not promoted to photon or gluon prediction unless a U(1)/color identity split and active benchmark contracts also exist.
