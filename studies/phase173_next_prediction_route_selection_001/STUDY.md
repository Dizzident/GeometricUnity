# Phase 173 Next Prediction Route Selection

This phase decides whether any currently available route can support the next defensible boson prediction.

It compares the exhausted W/Z absolute bridge route, the Higgs scalar-source route, and the photon/gluon massless-gauge route. The phase records diagnostic prediction attempts where the numerical observable exists, but rejects promotion unless the target-independent identity, source, stability, and benchmark contracts are all present.
