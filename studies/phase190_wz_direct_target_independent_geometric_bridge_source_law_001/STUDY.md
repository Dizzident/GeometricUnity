# Phase190 W/Z Direct Target-Independent Geometric Bridge-Source Law

This standalone study evaluates a branch-local candidate law for the remaining W/Z bridge-source gap:

`B_b,k(i,j) = <psi_b,i, delta D_omega[eta_b,k] psi_b,j>`.

For the P172 best W/Z-like fermion pair `4 -> 6`, the study reads Phase12 geometry, spinor metadata, boson registry entries, boson mode signatures, boson mode vectors, Phase91 branch-stability-promoted fermion mode vectors, and Phase12 variation matrices. It evaluates every boson candidate on both sibling backgrounds and ranks the resulting matrix-element magnitudes by sibling-background spread.

No W/Z target masses, target-implied raw amplitudes, or target residuals are used in construction or ranking. The output is evidence for a branch-local candidate law only, not a theorem.
