# Phase 534 — nested control battery

This phase runs three prospectively frozen controls in order: independent
Gaussian direct draws, a free-field HMC baseline, and HMC on the registered
theta-zero SD2 witness ray checked against deterministic nested quadrature and
an integration-by-parts identity. Each control has an independent failure
terminal. Passing the reduced controls does not validate the complete lattice
action, dynamical theta, or production sampling.
