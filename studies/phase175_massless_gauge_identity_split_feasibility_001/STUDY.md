# Phase 175 Massless Gauge Identity Split Feasibility

This phase checks whether the protected massless subspace can be split into known photon and gluon identities from current local artifacts.

It reads the Phase12 boson mode-signature curvature records, computes gauge-component energy fractions, and audits whether the available gauge basis can supply a target-independent U(1) photon identity or color-octet gluon identity. It is intentionally conservative: numerical masslessness is not enough unless the identity split and benchmark contracts exist.
