# Phase499: Phase456 retained empirical-noise information audit

This deterministic, read-only A11 phase exact-binds the committed Phase456
retained production artifact and the Phase496 census. It inventories only
finite retained values and aligned delete-block summaries. It labels what is
directly reconstructable at the stored temporal extent, what is confined to
that extent, and what is unavailable for calibrating a prospective larger
temporal extent.

The phase does not infer a temporal covariance kernel, extrapolate an
autocorrelation time, or treat aggregate delete-block values as raw
configuration samples. Its finite empirical envelopes are descriptive bounds
over the committed stored summaries, not a production noise or power model.

No sampler, HMC workload, benchmark, or new acquisition is run. The artifact
is not modified or reinterpreted, and the phase supplies no authority for a
Phase481 pack, Phase458 evaluation, source-contract application, physical-unit
calibration, or promoted mass claim.
