# Phase487: Independent SO(3) Haar Measure Control

This exploration-lane phase independently checks the normalized axis-angle
Haar law on SO(3). It uses a standalone deterministic quadrature and a local
quaternion-sandwich construction; it has no project reference to Phase450 and
does not import that phase's helpers.

The battery checks the analytic radial CDF and moments, character
orthogonality, rotation-matrix moments, invariance under fixed left and right
composition, and the identity and angle-pi boundaries. Its result cannot
author an external ruling, satisfy Phase458, authorize production, or support
a physical-unit claim.
