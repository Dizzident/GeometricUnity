# Phase120 Analytic Variation Measure Consistency

This phase validates the target-independent connection-mode-to-Dirac-variation amplitude measure requested by Phase119.

It compares the replay analytic variation matrix against persisted Phase12 finite-difference Dirac variation matrices for the W/Z source modes. The audit fits a target-independent scalar between the two operators and checks whether the residual and W/Z scale spread are small enough to promote the analytic amplitude measure before another absolute W/Z projection rerun.
