# Phase128 Fermion SU(2) Generator Sector Observable

This study tests the next target-blind fermion sector identity candidate after P127.

P127 showed that raw SU(2) gauge-basis energy fractions are mixed. P128 instead computes SU(2) adjoint generator moments for the repaired quality fermion modes:

- `<T_a>` generator expectations
- generator polarization magnitude
- generator variances and quadratic Casimir expectation
- `T3` eigenbasis fractions in the `m=-1,0,+1` basis

The observable is promotable only if all quality modes have a dominant `T3` eigenbasis sector and coherent generator polarization. It still requires nontrivial chirality or conjugation evidence before a W charged-current rule can be claimed.
