# Phase 183 Massless Sector Invariant Prediction

This phase resolves the photon/gluon masslessness blocker at the level of the contracted observable only.

The earlier massless-sector phases correctly refused to claim a target-independent photon U(1) identity or color-octet identity. However, the active photon and gluon benchmark contracts are not identity observables; they are zero masslessness-indicator contracts. If the entire protected gauge sector is numerically massless, gauge-leak-free, and dimension-stable across sibling backgrounds, then any later U(1)/color decomposition inside that sector inherits the same zero masslessness indicator.

This phase therefore promotes only the scalar masslessness indicator for photon and gluon. It does not claim a photon identity split, a color-octet identity split, confinement phenomenology, or individual registry-mode stability.
